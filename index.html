Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data FriendMap collects, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights regarding your personal information. FriendMap is operated as an independent app and is available in Ireland.

👤 Who We Are

FriendMap is a location-based social app that allows users to discover, create, and join local communities, chat with members, and organise real-world meetups. The app is developed and maintained independently.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at the email address listed at the bottom of this page.

📋 What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you use FriendMap:

  • Account information — your username, email address, date of birth, and profile picture (optional)
  • Location data — your approximate location, used only to show nearby communities on the map. We do not store your precise location or track your movements.
  • Messages and content — messages you send in community chats, meetup suggestions, and votes
  • Community activity — communities you create or join, and any meetups you participate in
  • Push notification token — a device token used to send you notifications about meetup reminders (via Expo)
  • Reports — if you submit a report about a user or community, we store the reason and any description you provide

🎯 Why We Collect It

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show you communities near your location on the map
  • To enable real-time chat within communities
  • To facilitate meetup planning and voting
  • To send push notifications for meetup reminders
  • To enforce our community guidelines through reporting and moderation
  • To verify you are at least 18 years of age

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to any third party.

🗄️ How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using the following services:

  • MongoDB Atlas — stores your account information, messages, community data, and reports on secure cloud servers
  • Cloudinary — stores profile pictures and community avatars as images on a secure content delivery network

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and WebSocket Secure (WSS) connections. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all of your personal data, messages, and uploaded images are permanently deleted.

📍 Location Data

FriendMap requests access to your device location solely to centre the map on your position and show nearby communities. We do not store your precise GPS coordinates, track your location over time, or share your location with other users.

Location permission is optional — you can use FriendMap without granting location access, though the map will default to a general view of Ireland.

👶 Age Requirement

FriendMap is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We collect your date of birth at registration solely to verify this requirement. Users under 18 are not permitted to use the app, and any account found to belong to a minor will be deleted.

🔔 Push Notifications

If you grant permission, FriendMap may send you push notifications to remind you of upcoming meetups you have joined. These notifications are sent via Expo's push notification service. Your device token is stored securely and used only for this purpose. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — you can view your account information in the app at any time
  • Correction — you can update your username and profile picture in User Settings
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the User Settings screen. This action is immediate and irreversible.
  • Data requests — you can contact us to request a copy of your data or ask any questions about how your data is used

🛡️ Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS), and access to our database is restricted and protected by authentication.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of FriendMap after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
